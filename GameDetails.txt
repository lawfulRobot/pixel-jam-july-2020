simple duel
	you have 5 units
	you have XX health
	each turn draw 5 cards
		cards are either attack or block
		there are 3 card tiers
		each tier deals or blocks 1,2,3 damage
	assign 1 card to each of the units
	combat is then executed
	when an attack deals damage you lose health
	the units don't die, instead damage is dealt to the player
	if your health reaches 0 you lose
